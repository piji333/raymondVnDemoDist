TrungVan Complex Building
Located on the Southwest suburban area of Hanoi, Trung Van Intracom Residential and Commercial Complex aims to blow a new wind through the old and deteriorated apartment blocks by offering a variety of high-end residential facilities including outdoor garden restaurants, a swimming pool, shopping centers and spacious apartments with rooftop gardens. The complex consists of two parts facing each other to form a central garden on the ground floor, one 25-storey residential block and one 5-storey commercial building featured by a rooftop pool and restaurant.
